My Bio

Hello, my name is Stephan and I love video games. I gravitated towards video games at a very early age because I just found everything about them fascinating. I can remember getting my first video game when I was 4 years old; it was Scooby Doo: Mystery Mayhem for the Game Boy Advance SP and I thought it was the coolest thing. As I grew older I played all sorts of games on many different consoles and on PC. I am now currently 25 years old and have played many different generations of consoles/ video games. Some of favorites include the Game Boy Advance, Game Cube, PSP/ PlayStation Portable, PlayStation 2, PlayStation 3, and the Xbox 360. I have so many fond memories of playing with family and friends from as early 4 years old until now. You can find a few of my favorite genres and games if you navigate to the Genres page. Hope you enjoy!
