Call of Duty: Black Ops

Black Ops was the first FPS game I played that was set in the past and tied into real events that took place. It was set in the 1960's during the Cold War and followed really cool protagonists that you played as in their campaign game mode. It also had a great multiplayer game mode with cool new features such as the dolphin dive and was one of the most played FPS games of all time. I am a big fan of map design especially when it comes to these fast paced shooter games and Treyarch maps traditionally have a great flow because of their three lane map design. The game was released in 2010 by Treyarch.
