Super Mario Strikers

Super Mario Strikers is a Mario soccer game! This game is just a very goofy but super fun game to play with friends. It has the iconic characters that we all know and love and you get to create a team with these characters. Not only do you get to play as these characters but you can play these soccer matches in arenas with themes of famous zones/maps in the franchise such as Luigi's Mansion.
