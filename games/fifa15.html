FIFA 15

FIFA 15 is my favorite sports game of all time. It was at a point where the game was not yet as realistic as it is now but that's what made it fun. Being able to perform ridiculous plays with players who you really shouldn't have been able to run these plays with. I can still remember being in high school playing with all my friends at the time because FIFA has a game mode where you can play on a team online but only as your created character in your assigned position. This game will always be the best installment of the franchise just because I have so many fond memories playing it.
