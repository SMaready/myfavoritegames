First Person Shooters

One of the most known and beloved genres in the video game community is FPS. FPS games have been part of the gaming industry since the 1970's with games like Maze War but did not become very popular until the 1990's when games like Doom and Quake were released. My introduction to first person shooters was Call of Duty: Modern Warfare. I then got into other series such as Halo with the release of Halo 3.
